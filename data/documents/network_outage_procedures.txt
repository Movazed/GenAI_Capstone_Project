Prodapt - Network Outage Response Procedures
Internal Reference Document (Customer-Facing Summary)

INCIDENT CLASSIFICATION
CRITICAL: Complete loss of service affecting >10,000 subscribers or entire metro region
HIGH: Significant degradation affecting 1,000-10,000 subscribers or multiple towers
MEDIUM: Single tower or sector outage affecting <1,000 subscribers
LOW: Performance degradation without complete service loss

INITIAL RESPONSE CHECKLIST
1. NOC validates alarm from monitoring system (SNMP traps, customer reports, social media)
2. Assign incident commander and open ticket in ServiceNow (INC prefix)
3. Identify affected towers by region, technology (LTE/5G), and estimated customer count
4. Notify regional field teams and activate backup power assessment for affected sites
5. Post initial status to status.prodapt.com within 30 minutes of confirmed outage

COMMON ROOT CAUSES
- Fiber backhaul cut (construction, vehicle accident): 35% of CRITICAL incidents
- Power outage at tower site without functioning generator: 25%
- RAN software bug after patch deployment: 15%
- Weather (lightning, ice loading on antennas): 15%
- Equipment hardware failure (RRU, baseband unit): 10%

CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION TEMPLATES
CRITICAL outage: SMS blast to affected ZIP codes within 1 hour
"We are aware of a service disruption in [REGION]. Our teams are working to restore
service. Updates at status.prodapt.com/inc/[INC_ID]"

RESTORATION VERIFICATION
Before closing incident:
- Run drive tests in affected area
- Confirm tower status = OPERATIONAL in NOC dashboard
- Verify customer complaint volume returns to baseline
- Document root cause and preventive actions in post-incident review

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE
- Generator fuel level checks: monthly at all sites
- Battery backup tests: quarterly
- Software patch staging: 5% canary deployment before full rollout
- Fiber route inspections: semi-annual in high-risk construction zones

CUSTOMER IMPACT ESTIMATION
Use formula: affected_subscribers = sum(sector_capacity * outage_duration_hours)
for SLA credit calculations. Refer to sla_policy.txt for credit eligibility rules.
