Prodapt - International Roaming Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

OVERVIEW
Prodapt offers international roaming in 180+ countries through partner networks.
Roaming charges and data speeds vary by destination zone and your home plan tier.

ROAMING ZONES
Zone A (Canada, Mexico): Included at no extra charge on Unlimited Plus and Business Pro plans.
  Data usage counts against your domestic allowance at 1:1 ratio.

Zone B (Western Europe, UK, Japan, South Korea, Australia):
  - Unlimited Plus: 10 GB/month included, then $10/GB
  - Standard Unlimited: $0.25/min voice, $0.10/SMS, $12/GB data
  - Business Pro: 25 GB/month included

Zone C (Latin America excluding Mexico, Eastern Europe, Middle East, Africa):
  - All plans: $0.35/min voice, $0.15/SMS, $15/GB data
  - Travel Pass available: $10/day for 500 MB high-speed data + unlimited low-speed

Zone D (Rest of World including remote islands):
  - Pay-as-you-go only: $1.50/min voice, $0.50/SMS, $20/GB data
  - Travel Pass not available in Zone D countries

TRAVEL PASS ACTIVATION
Enable Travel Pass before departure via:
- Prodapt mobile app > Settings > International Roaming
- SMS "TRAVEL ON" to 611
- Customer portal at my.prodapt.com

Travel Pass activates on first network registration abroad and charges one daily fee
per 24-hour period in the local time zone of the visited country.

DATA ROAMING SAFEGUARDS
All accounts have a default $100/month roaming spend cap. Customers may raise or lower
this cap in the app. When 80% of the cap is reached, SMS and email alerts are sent.

DEVICE COMPATIBILITY
Your device must support the frequency bands used in your destination. Prodapt
recommends checking band compatibility at prodapt.com/roaming-check before travel.
5G roaming is available in Zone A and B countries on select flagship devices only.

BILLING DISPUTES FOR ROAMING
Roaming charges appear on the bill cycle when usage occurred, not when you returned home.
Dispute roaming charges within 60 days by providing travel dates and destination.
