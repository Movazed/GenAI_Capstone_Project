Prodapt - Device Upgrade and Trade-In Policy

ELIGIBILITY
Postpaid customers may upgrade devices when:
- 50% of device payment plan is completed, OR
- 24 months have passed since activation, OR
- Device is fully paid off

Business accounts follow contract-specific upgrade schedules (typically 24 or 36 months).

UPGRADE OPTIONS
1. Early Upgrade Program ($35 upgrade fee + return current device in good condition)
2. Buyout remaining device balance and purchase new device at full retail or installment
3. Trade-in credit applied to new device (see trade-in values below)

TRADE-IN VALUES (Sample - varies by condition)
iPhone 14 Pro (excellent): up to $450 credit
iPhone 13 (good): up to $280 credit
Samsung Galaxy S23 (excellent): up to $380 credit
Google Pixel 7 (good): up to $200 credit

Condition grades:
- Excellent: no cracks, fully functional, battery health >85%
- Good: minor scratches, fully functional, battery health >80%
- Fair: visible wear, functional with screen damage under 1 inch
- Poor: not eligible for trade-in credit (recycle program available)

NEW DEVICE INSTALLMENT
0% APR for 24 or 36 months on qualified credit.
Down payment may be required for customers with credit score below 650.

eSIM AND NUMBER TRANSFER
Upgrades preserve your phone number. eSIM profiles can be transferred via the Prodapt
app without visiting a store. Physical SIM swaps available free at retail locations.

RETURN POLICY
New devices may be returned within 14 days if unused and in original packaging.
Restocking fee of $50 applies after 14 days or if device shows usage.

INSURANCE
Prodapt Device Protection ($15/month) covers accidental damage, loss, and theft with
$99 deductible. Required for Early Upgrade Program participation.

Contact: upgrades@prodapt.com | Visit any Prodapt store with valid ID
