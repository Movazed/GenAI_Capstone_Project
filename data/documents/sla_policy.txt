Prodapt - Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy
Version 3.2 | Effective January 2026

1. NETWORK AVAILABILITY COMMITMENT
Prodapt guarantees 99.5% network uptime for postpaid business and enterprise customers
on our 4G LTE and 5G NR networks. Consumer prepaid plans are covered at 99.0% uptime.

2. OUTAGE CREDIT ELIGIBILITY
Customers may receive a service credit when a verified network outage exceeds:
- 4 consecutive hours for business plans (credit: one day of monthly service fee)
- 8 consecutive hours for consumer plans (credit: 50% of one day of service fee)
Credits are applied automatically within two billing cycles when the outage is logged in our
Network Operations Center (NOC) system with severity HIGH or CRITICAL.

3. MEAN TIME TO REPAIR (MTTR)
- CRITICAL outages (complete regional loss): target restoration within 4 hours
- HIGH severity (degraded service >50% customers in a cell sector): target 8 hours
- MEDIUM severity (localized tower issues): target 24 hours

4. EXCLUSIONS
SLA credits do not apply for outages caused by:
- Customer device failure or misconfiguration
- Scheduled maintenance announced 72 hours in advance
- Force majeure events (hurricanes, earthquakes, civil unrest)
- Third-party backhaul provider failures beyond Prodapt control

5. PERFORMANCE METRICS
Business customers may request quarterly reports showing:
- Average latency (target: <40ms on 5G, <60ms on LTE)
- Packet loss (target: <0.5%)
- Jitter (target: <15ms)

6. ESCALATION
If an outage exceeds MTTR targets, enterprise customers may escalate to their assigned
Customer Success Manager. Reference your account number and the NOC incident ID.

Contact: sla@prodapt.com | NOC Hotline: 1-800-NOC-HELP
