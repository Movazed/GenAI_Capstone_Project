Prodapt - Billing Disputes and Adjustments Policy

1. DISPUTE WINDOW
Customers must report billing errors within 90 days of the statement date.
Disputes submitted after 90 days may be reviewed at Prodapt discretion but are
not guaranteed adjustment.

2. VALID DISPUTE CATEGORIES
Eligible for investigation and potential credit:
- Duplicate charges for the same service period
- Charges for services not activated or already cancelled
- Incorrect plan tier applied after documented plan change
- Roaming charges when Travel Pass was active (requires proof of activation timestamp)
- Equipment charges after verified return with RMA number
- Taxes calculated on incorrect address

NOT eligible for automatic credit:
- Usage overages when usage logs confirm consumption
- Late payment fees when payment was received after due date
- Promotional pricing expiration (customers notified 30 days prior)
- Third-party content purchases (app store, premium SMS)

3. INVESTIGATION TIMELINE
- Standard disputes: resolution within 10 business days
- Complex disputes involving partner carriers: up to 30 business days
- During investigation, disputed amount may be placed on hold (not sent to collections)

4. CREDIT LIMITS
Automatic credits without manager approval: up to $50 per incident
Supervisor approval required: $50.01 - $200
Director approval required: above $200

5. REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION
For roaming disputes: travel dates, destination, Travel Pass activation screenshot
For duplicate charges: statement showing both charges with transaction IDs
For cancellation disputes: cancellation confirmation number and date

6. PAYMENT DURING DISPUTE
Customers must pay undisputed portions of the bill by the due date to avoid late fees.
Disputed amounts on hold do not accrue late fees.

7. ESCALATION PATH
Tier 1 Billing Support -> Billing Resolution Team -> Ombudsman (for amounts >$500)

Contact Billing Resolution: 1-800-BILL-HELP or billing.disputes@prodapt.com
